# V1.7 Brain-Parity Sprint — research-driven reshape (2026-05-03)

## Goal

Take nullalis brain from "graph that exists" to **Obsidian-level graph per user, but auto-extracted and AI-aware**. The latter half is our structural advantage; the former half is what's missing.

### What other PKM tools teach

- **Tana** — Supertags = typed nodes; closest to our typed-edge model. Validates that typed structure is the differentiator.
- **Roam** — Reviewed as "graph unusable" despite strong backlinking. Lesson: backlinking ≠ graph utility; the VIEW matters.
- **Logseq** — Block-level granularity adds noise. Lesson: per-fact resolution (which we have) is right.

### What AI memory systems lack

- **Graphiti** — Has the graph data model (we adopted it) but **no first-class viz UI** — Neo4j Browser is the de-facto fallback. Our `/brain/*` is a real differentiator.
- **Mem0** — Thin triples + parallel vector store. We are AHEAD on edge richness.
